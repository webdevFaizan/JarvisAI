OpenAI response for :   what is open ai 


 
OpenAI is a research laboratory dedicated to the study and development of Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). The laboratory was founded in December 2015 with a $1 billion commitment from tech titans including Microsoft, Amazon, and Tesla. OpenAI's research efforts are focused on developing a general, human-like artificial intelligence (AI) that can tackle any task or problem, and its ultimate goal is to create safe AI that can benefit humanity.
